St. Catherine Institute of Technology

is an institution accredited by TESDA that offers Technicals and Vocational Educational and Training Programs (TVET) under the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) and the Commission on Higher Education (CHED).

St. Catherine Institute of Technology shall provide quality technical-vocational education in skills training for the youth of tomorrow imbued with work ethics making them self reliant for gainful employment and entrepreneurship.

Our Working team is composed of competent individuals whose expertise and track records in the field of education delivers the art of educational as well as professional services. They are committed to our creed in the advancement of knowledge for improving human life . Much more, the schools is equipped to bring fore the state of the art innovations and the latest trend in technologies to shape the technical. electronics and computer fields.
